Job Search

One search across the web’s best job boards — private sector, remote, and federal roles, together.

In development

01 Overview

Stop checking the same five sites

Job hunting usually means running the same query across a handful of different sites, each with its own layout, filters, and quirks — and still wondering what you missed. This app brings openings from several major sources into a single, unified search, so you can see relevant roles across the market in one consistent place.

Each source covers a different slice of the job market. Searching them together gives a fuller picture than any one board can on its own.

02 Sources

Where the listings come from

  • Adzuna Broad coverage of openings across many industries and employers.
  • USAJobs The official source for United States federal government positions.
  • Remotive Curated remote-first roles for people who want to work from anywhere.
  • The Muse Roles from companies with detailed profiles, so you learn about the workplace, not just the title.

03 The idea

One place, less busywork

By unifying these sources, the app aims to make a job search faster and less repetitive — search once, see results from across the market, and spend your energy on applications instead of tab-juggling.
