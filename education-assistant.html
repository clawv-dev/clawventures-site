Education Assistant

Less busywork, more learning — an AI helper for students and educators.

In development

01 Overview

More time for the part that matters

Teaching and studying are full of small, repetitive tasks that eat into the time meant for actual learning. This assistant is designed to take that load off — helping students and educators handle everyday classroom and study work so they can focus on understanding the material, not managing it.

02 The idea

An assistant built around the school day

The goal is a helpful, dependable companion for the routines of learning — organizing materials, staying on top of what’s coming up, and getting quick help when a question comes up — designed to fit naturally into how students and teachers already work.

Note: This product is in an early stage. Its name and feature set are still being shaped, and this page reflects its direction rather than a final specification.
