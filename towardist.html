Towardist

Your AI goal partner — turning good intentions into real, sustained progress.

In development Web, iOS & Android

01 Overview

Goals are easy to set and hard to sustain

Most goals don’t fail at the starting line — they fade a few weeks in, once the initial motivation wears off. Towardist is built for exactly that gap. Instead of being another checklist that waits for you to remember it, it pairs you with an AI coach that understands your specific goal, breaks it into achievable milestones, and keeps momentum alive.

The result feels less like a productivity tool and more like having a knowledgeable partner in your corner — one that adapts to what you’re actually working on.

02 Features

What Towardist does

  • Goals & milestones Define a meaningful goal and Towardist helps shape it into concrete, trackable milestones, so the path forward is always clear.
  • A personalized AI coach Each goal gets a coaching persona tailored to its domain — fitness, learning, career, creative work — so guidance feels relevant instead of generic.
  • Focused coaching sessions Short check-ins guide you through a simple loop: get a cue, take action, record the outcome, and receive your next step.

03 Who it’s for

Anyone with a goal worth finishing

Whether it’s getting fit, learning a skill, changing careers, or finishing a creative project, Towardist is for people who know what they want and need help staying with it — built for the long middle stretch where most good intentions quietly stall.
