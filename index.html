01 About

An independent studio with a focused portfolio

Claw Ventures LLC is a Maryland limited liability company founded in 2023. We design, build, and operate a small portfolio of consumer applications, with a common thread: pairing dependable engineering with AI where it genuinely helps, and leaving it out where it doesn’t.

Rather than spreading thin, we build a handful of apps end to end — product, engineering, and support — and stand behind each one. The products below are in active development ahead of public release.
